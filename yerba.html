Mission Statement

Reach deep into yerba mate culture and you'll discover people have long gathered to imbibe mate to awaken the mind, perform extrodinary feats and to exchange confidences. Even Yari, the mythical goddess of mate, decrees it the symbol of friendship. We've always believed there was a magical tree in the rainforest with powers to unite and energize. Come to life with this high energy infusion brewed from the mighty, emerald green mate leaves.



About the Brand

The Power of Yerbification was founded in 1979 with the goal in mind that there was a life beyond those who drink coffee. Fueled by those at Woodstock, The Power of Yerbification was intended to keep those up who enjoy the best things in life all day and night.

The Power of Yerbification is a store where you can come in to learn about the culture of Yerba Mate as well as expand your pallet in our taste testing class. It is an educational experience for all ages and to further idulge your senses. Who knows, maybe someday you will switch out your traditional caffine go-tos for Yerba Mate. The Power of Yerbification is a great place to start.
