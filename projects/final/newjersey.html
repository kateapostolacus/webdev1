June 18th- Day 1
For my first day back in Jersey, per tradition, the first order of business
is to get a bagel. Once those matters are taken care of, it is time to meet
up with my friends. I only get to see my hometown friends once or twice
a year so we make sure those moments count.
June 19th- Day 2
June 19th happens to be Father's Day, and I will conveniently be staying
with my dad. On Father's Day (since I will be legal) I can't wait to
take him out for once and do all the things that dads enjoy doing. My dad
really enjoys biking, bowling, and breweries.
June 20th- Day 3
Being from New Jersey, I do happen to have close proximity to New York City.
After growing up in New Jersey, I also have a lot of friends that go to school
in the city as well, including my best friend, Emma. On Monday, we are thinking
of heading into the city so she can show me all her favorite spots.

I totally forgot to mention I live right near the beach, so Day 4 will be a beach

I still have a lot of friends that work at the beach. Did you know you have to pay
to get on the beach in New Jersey? It's what I deserve since I don't know how to pump gas.
