June 13th- Day 1
For our first day in Italy, I fly into Rome around 1pm. Once I
arrive in Rome, my first excursion will be getting to our Airbnb,
and maybe getting a pizza and a drink. Then around 5pm, my best friend
Sabina's flight will get in from America. We will get settled in then go out
for the night at a local restaurant and club.
June 14th- Day 2
Day 2 is touring day as we will wake up and make a small breakfast,
then begin our tourist things. Starting at Campidoglio, Foro Romano,
Colosseum, MACRO museum, and Porto Pia. Around Porto Pia is
where we will get lunch. After lunch we will hit up the Trevi Fountain,
Spanish Steps, and end with Villa Medici. We will go back to the Airbnb
and refresh before dinner and then ending the night with Lizzie Maguire.
June 15th- Day 3
Day 3 is next and we are heading to the Vatican City. The Vatican
is home to the Sistine Chapel and is one of the smallest countries.
After we explore the Vatican, we will then head to the neighboring
Trastevere area. This area was recommened to me by my cousin
who studied abroad in Rome. While in Trastevere, we will stop by
the Giardino del Teatro, which are gardens in Rome. Lastly, if we still
have energy, we will hit the town at night for clubbing.
June 16th- Day 4
Day 4 is our last full day in Italy and we are thinking of an excursion
to either the beach or Pompeii. The beach we are thinking of Il Gabbiano
which is a nice beach. We could also go to Pompeii but that is a bit of a
trek, and I get carsick. After our day of excursions, the night will include
a chill night in packing and getting ready for the next day.
June 17th- Day 5
For our final moments in Italy, it is time for us to say goodbye to our
Italian adventure. Sabina and I will be flying from Rome to Lisbon for
our 4 hour layover and then off to New Jersey where I will reunite with
my dad and friends from home!
